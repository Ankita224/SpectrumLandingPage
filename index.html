About us

SPECTRUM, the technical society of the College of Engineering and Technology, Bhubaneswar, a place for all technical enthusiasts to learn, discover and innovate new things in the field of Technology and Design with having three wings i.e, Hardware, Software and Design. Just as the literal meaning of the phenomenon "Spectrum", students of CET consist of different skills within them, and Spectrum acts as a prism, Spectrum recognizes their skills helps them to strengthen them. This club was established by " Bikram Keshari Panda (2015 batch)”. Spectrum got its recognition in the year 2016 and got its workspace, funding, and other facilities.

Hardware

The hardware team of this club focuses on Visi, Embedded systems and PCB desgin. We have developed many projects with implementaion and testing of circuits, components, systems, etc.

Software

Our software tam is continuosly striving for growth in the domain of Web development,Mobile App development, Machine learning and Data science.

Design

The Design wing of our club has proven their creativity in UI/UX poster designing. We believe visual communicstion is major tool to enhance varius creative project
